About This Property

The Pali Hill Loft is a one-of-a-kind duplex that perfectly blends industrial aesthetics with warm, livable design. Located on a quiet lane in Pali Village, you're surrounded by boutique cafés, art galleries, and some of Mumbai's best dining.

The ground floor features an open-plan living and dining area with a fully equipped kitchen. Upstairs, two bedrooms offer peaceful retreats, while the private rooftop is perfect for morning yoga or evening cocktails.
